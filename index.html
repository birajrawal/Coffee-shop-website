Welcome to Aroma Espresso Sip Cafe

Fresh coffee, all-day breakfast, light lunches & sweet treats—rooted in Mediterranean simplicity.

We start with quality: responsibly sourced beans, seasonal produce, extra-virgin olive oil, and herbs that brighten every bite. Enjoy fluffy omelettes, hearty shakshuka, crisp salads, artisanal sandwiches, and pastry classics baked daily. Whether you’re grabbing a morning cappuccino or planning a cozy brunch, we’ve got a seat and a smile waiting.
